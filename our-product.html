Our Product


Wacamole is more than just food – it's an explosion of Mexican street flavor, right in the heart of Nashville.

Everything we serve is made with fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and a twist of local creativity. From crunchy tacos to loaded burritos, our food hits hard and hugs your soul.


Here's what makes us different:

  • Street-style tacos with options like al pastor, chipotle chicken, carnitas, and our veggie-friendly creations – all served on soft corn tortillas made to order.
  • Burritos & bowls packed with rice, beans, cheese, fresh veggies, salsas, and your choice of protein – perfect for lunch breaks or a post-party fix.
  • Melty quesadillas and loaded nachos that come with homemade guacamole, sour cream, and our signature salsa trio.
  • Weekly specials and secret menu items that reward the true fans.
  • Vegan and gluten-free options available because everyone deserves to enjoy the sabor.

Planning something big?


Wacamole offers custom catering for office parties, birthdays, music festivals, and any event that deserves a spicy touch.

We're not just serving food. We're serving culture. One bite at a time.
